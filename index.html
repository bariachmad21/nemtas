ABOUT THE CLUB

More than just a team, we are a family.

Our Mission

The Nemtas Basketball Club is dedicated to developing student-athletes of character. We provide a structured, competitive, and supportive environment where players can hone their basketball skills while learning the invaluable life lessons of teamwork, perseverance, leadership, and sportsmanship.

Our Home Court

All practices and home games are held at the official SMKN 6 South Tangerang gymnasium. We take pride in our facility and the incredible support from our school community.
